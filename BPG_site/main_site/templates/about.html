About BPG

Big Picture Grip is a grip rental house serving New Mexico and the southwest. We are a family
business, focused on providing production ready grip trucks, with up to date equipment and
organized layouts. BPG's founder, Evan Nelson, has over 20 years in the business as a grip,
technocrane operator and key grip. We have teamed up with the very best crews here in NM,
and look forward to providing your next shoot with with top quality grip support.
